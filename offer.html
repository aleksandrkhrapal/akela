Overview

Kleos is offering a remarkable shift in global geo-located intelligence gathering capability from Space. We are able to ‘listen’ and ‘locate’ radio signals from, for example; Satphone, Mobile Phone, Maritime VHF, Ships of undesirable users such as; drugs/people smugglers, terrorists, pirates, illegal fishermen and also from those in need of search and rescue.

Kleos Space will deploy Space based technology and assets that can locate and track any device transmitting radio frequencies, enabling the provision of global monitoring and tracking. This intelligence will be provided as a DaaS (Data As A Service) to Government Ministries as well as Defense, Border Security and commercial organisations.

Kleos has a first mover advantage to penetrate the $26B global Intelligence Surveillance Reconnaisance (ISR) market, and provide the DaaS to NATO countries.

Recent investments from the UK Space Agency, European Space Agency, UK Ministry of Defence, and the Luxembourg Government de-risk and underpin the validity of Kleos , with the Luxembourg Government also expressing an interest in co-investing along side future funding rounds such as the IPO.
